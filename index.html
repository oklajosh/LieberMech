A Family Tradition Since 1949

When it comes to indoor comfort and air quality, you and your family deserve only the best. Most importantly, you also want a quality air conditioning and heating system that provides value as well as comfort. Lieber Mechanical is proud of over 60 years of high quality service in the Oklahoma City metro area, providing residential and commercial customers alike with complete sales and installation of heating and air conditioning systems. Plus if you suffer from allergies, Lieber Mechanical offers excellent air quality systems at affordable prices.

Lieber Mechanical services all makes and models and offers a "Planned Service Agreement" to keep your home system running efficiently and cost effectively. Lieber Mechanical is family owned and operated and is a proud member of the Air Conditioning Contractors of America, International Ground Source Heat Pump Association, and a Lead Safe Certified Firm.
